Grandma's Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is satisfying and filling that it is worth the extra effort!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
