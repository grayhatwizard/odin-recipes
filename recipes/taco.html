Tex-Mex Tacos

Quick and full of flavor! You can adjust this recipe with parts that you prefer.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Black Beans
  • Shredded, Mexican Cheese
  • Sliced Olives
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Jalepeno Pepper
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Avocados
  • Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Mix rice, black beans, cheese, olives, and jalepenos into the chicken
  4. Warm tortillas on a separate skillet
  5. Fill each tortilla with chicken and rice mixture
  6. Fry filled tortillas and serve with desired toppings
